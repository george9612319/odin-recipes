Pan-Fried Asparagus

Learn how to cook asparagus on the stove by pan-frying it in butter with garlic for a delicious Italian-style side dish.

Description

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed

Steps

  1. Butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add olive oil, salt, and pepper; cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Add asparagus and cook until fork-tender, turning asparagus often to ensure even cooking, about 10 minutes.
